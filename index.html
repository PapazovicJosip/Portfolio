About Me

My name is Josip Papazović, born on October 3, 1999, in the small town of Našice, located in the Slavonia region of Croatia. I completed my primary education at Dora Pejačević Elementary School in Našice and later graduated from Isidor Kršnjavi High School, where I studied as an Agricultural Technician – General Program for four years.

After finishing school, I joined the army and served as an active soldier for two years in the 7th Guards Brigade "Puma". Following my military service, I briefly worked in the metal industry for a private company. Later, I moved to Switzerland, where I started a family with my wife, and together we are raising our two children and building a new life.

I have always been curious about how technology works, which led me to pursue programming. As a teenager, I spent a lot of time playing video games with friends, which sparked my interest in how games and applications are created.

So far, I have completed:

  • Introduction to GPT and its Tools
  • Core Java Programming
  • HTML and CSS Fundamentals

Currently, I am enrolled in PHP Core Programming, and on November 1, 2025, I will begin studying Frontend JavaScript Development with AI at Link Group ITAcademy.

Outside of programming, I have a strong background in sports. I played football (soccer) for 13 years, trained in kickboxing for 2 years, MMA for 3 months, and Muay Thai for 5 months, sometimes practicing them in parallel.

This unique mix of discipline from the military, teamwork from sports, and curiosity for technology has shaped me into a motivated and dedicated individual, ready to grow further as a developer.
